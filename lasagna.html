Lasagna

Description

This seems to be the most American recipe for a lasagna I've ever read.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil
  6. Let the lasagna rest
