Shakshuka

Description

Shakshuka is the ultimate one-pan meal that feels indulgent yet incredibly simple to make. Originating from North Africa and popular across the Middle East, this vibrant dish features gently poached eggs nestled in a spiced tomato and bell pepper sauce. Recipe by vernabanana

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Onion and garlic
  • tomato products
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Red pepper
  • Black olives

Steps

  1. Cook the base
  2. Make the tomato sauce
  3. Create wells for the eggs
  4. Garnish and serve
